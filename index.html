FAQ

Common Questions About Porta-John Rentals

How much does it cost to rent a porta-john in the greater Charlotte area? +

Carolina Sanitation offers construction site portable restroom rentals starting at $115 per unit per month with no delivery or pickup fees (4-month minimum). Weekend event rentals are $165 for the weekend plus applicable sales tax.

Do you charge delivery or pickup fees for construction rentals? +

No. Our construction rental rate of $115 per unit per month includes delivery and pickup — no extra charges. The only requirement is a 4-month minimum commitment.

What areas do you deliver porta-johns to? +

We serve the greater Charlotte, NC area including Union County (Monroe, Indian Trail, Waxhaw, Wesley Chapel, Stallings), Mecklenburg County (Charlotte, Huntersville, Matthews, Mint Hill, Cornelius, Davidson), Cabarrus County (Concord, Kannapolis, Harrisburg), Gaston County (Gastonia, Belmont, Mount Holly), and Anson County.

Can I book and pay for a porta-john online for my event? +

Yes. Event rentals can be booked and paid for directly online via our secure Stripe checkout — $165 for the weekend plus applicable sales tax. You'll enter your event date and delivery address at checkout. Book your event now →

How far in advance should I book a portable restroom? +

For weekend events, we recommend booking at least 3–5 days in advance to ensure availability. For construction site rentals, contact us as early as possible — ideally a week or more before your project start date — so we can schedule delivery to fit your timeline.
