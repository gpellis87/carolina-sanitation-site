Privacy Policy

How Carolina Sanitation handles information submitted through this website for portable restroom rentals and inquiries.

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Information We Collect

When you contact us, request a quote, or submit a booking form, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, service address, event or job-site details, rental preferences, and any notes you choose to provide.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, confirm availability, schedule delivery or pickup, provide customer service, process bookings, and maintain basic business records.

Service Providers

This website may use third-party providers for form delivery, spam prevention, analytics, and payment processing. These providers may process information only as needed to provide their services. Payments are handled by the payment processor and Carolina Sanitation does not store full card details on this website.

Cookies and Analytics

The site may use basic cookies or privacy-focused analytics to understand website performance and improve usability. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers, contractors, or authorities when needed to operate the business, complete a rental request, comply with law, or protect our rights.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no website or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

Contact
