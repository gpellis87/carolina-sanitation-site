When a Standard Rental Isn't Enough

Some situations call for a porta-john that stays put — not for a weekend, not for a construction contract, but indefinitely. A rural property without plumbing, a hunting camp on private land, a storage facility that needs restroom access — these are the kinds of needs that don't fit a standard short-term rental.

Carolina Sanitation handles these placements with the same reliability as our other services: we deliver the unit, set up a regular servicing schedule, and you don't have to think about it again.

FAQ

Long-Term Rental Questions

What is a permanent portable restroom placement? +
A permanent placement is a long-term portable restroom rental that stays on-site indefinitely — for rural properties, farms, remote land, or any location that needs ongoing restroom access without traditional plumbing. We deliver, service on a regular schedule, and the unit stays as long as you need it.
How is long-term rental priced? +
Pricing is custom — it depends on your location, how many units you need, how often we service them, and access requirements. Contact us for a quote and we'll work out something fair for your situation.
What's the minimum commitment for a long-term rental? +
Long-term placements typically start at a 4-month minimum, similar to our construction rentals. After that, we can continue month-to-month. We're flexible — just talk to us about your timeline.
Can you service units in rural or hard-to-reach areas? +
Yes, within our service area. We serve rural properties throughout Union County, Cabarrus County, Gaston County, Anson County, and surrounding areas. When you request a quote, include your property address and any access details and we'll confirm serviceability.
