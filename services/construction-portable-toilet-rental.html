What's Included

  • Delivery to your job site address
  • Weekly cleaning and servicing
  • Pickup at end of contract
  • Hand sanitizer dispenser inside each unit
  • Ventilated, well-maintained units

The $115/month rate covers everything. The only condition is a 4-month minimum commitment — which works well for the typical construction timeline. If your project runs longer, just continue month-to-month.

How Many Units Does Your Site Need?

OSHA recommends at least one portable toilet per 20 workers for an 8-hour shift. For longer shifts or larger crews, plan on one per 10 workers. Here's a quick guide:

  • 1–20 workers → 1 unit minimum
  • 21–40 workers → 2 units
  • 41–60 workers → 3 units
  • Larger crews → contact us and we'll size it properly

When you submit your request, include your crew size and we'll recommend the right count for your site.

How to Get Started

Fill out the construction request form on our booking page — it takes about 2 minutes. Include your job site address, how many units you need, your expected start date, and project duration. We'll confirm availability and reach out to schedule delivery.

Construction Rental Questions

How much does construction porta-john rental cost? +
$115 per unit per month, all-inclusive. Delivery, weekly servicing, and pickup are all covered in that rate — no add-ons, no surprise fees. The 4-month minimum applies.
Are there any delivery or pickup fees? +
No. Delivery and pickup are included in the monthly rate as long as you meet the 4-month minimum commitment. There are no extra charges for those.
What is the minimum rental period? +
4 months. After the initial 4 months, the rental continues month-to-month until you no longer need the unit. Contact us when you're ready to end service and we'll schedule pickup.
How many porta-johns do I need for my crew? +
OSHA recommends one toilet per 20 workers for an 8-hour shift. For larger crews, plan on one per 10 workers. When you submit your request, tell us your crew size and we'll recommend the right number.
What areas do you serve for construction rentals? +
We deliver to construction sites throughout Union County, Mecklenburg County, Cabarrus County, Gaston County, Anson County, and surrounding areas in the greater Charlotte, NC region.
