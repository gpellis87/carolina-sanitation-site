How Many Units Do I Need?

A general guideline for outdoor events:

  • Up to 50 guests for 4 hours → 1 unit
  • 50–100 guests or all-day event → 2 units
  • 100–200 guests → 3–4 units
  • Alcohol being served → add one extra unit per 50 guests

When you book online you can adjust the quantity at checkout. Not sure? Call us and we'll help you size it right.

FAQ

Event Rental Questions

How much does event porta-john rental cost? +
$165 for the weekend plus applicable sales tax, which is calculated and added automatically at checkout. That rate covers delivery, the full weekend, and Monday pickup.
Can I book online? +
Yes — event rentals are booked and paid entirely online via secure Stripe checkout. The process takes about 2 minutes. You'll enter your event date, delivery address, and number of units, then pay instantly.
What if I need more than one unit? +
You can adjust the quantity at checkout on the Stripe booking page. The total updates automatically. For large events with 5+ units, call us at 704-956-1182 and we'll handle it directly.
When will the unit be delivered and picked up? +
We deliver Friday or Saturday before your event weekend. Pickup happens Monday morning. If you have a specific time window needed for delivery, note it when you book and we'll do our best to accommodate.
How far in advance do I need to book? +
We recommend booking at least 3–5 days before your event to guarantee availability. During busy summer weekends, booking further in advance is a good idea. Last-minute requests may be possible — call us to check.
